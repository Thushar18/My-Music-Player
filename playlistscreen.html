Jai Shri Ram

"Jai Shri Ram" is a song by Indian singers Ajay-Atul, taken from the Adipurush movie, 2023. The song was released to Youtube as the first single from Adipurush 2023 on April 6, 2023, by T-Series Films in partnership with Retrophiles. The First Single titled "Jai Shri Ram" was released in its short version on 6 April 2023 on the occasion of Hanuman Jayanti. The Full song was released on 20 May 2023 under the same name in Hindi and Telugu alongside dubbed versions(Tamil,Kannada and Malayalam).
